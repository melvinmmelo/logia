# Play Store — Full Description

Google Play allows up to **4,000 characters** for the full description. The draft
below is well under that limit and sticks to features that exist in the app today.

---

**Steward** is a simple, offline-first personal finance app for Android. It helps
you keep track of your money in one place — your cash and digital wallets, the
money coming in, the money going out, and the loans you owe or are owed.

No account sign-up. No cloud sync. No ads. Your data stays on your device.

## What you can do

- **Track multiple accounts.** Add wallets like Cash, GCash, Maya, a bank
  account, or a credit card. Each account shows its current balance based on
  your entries.
- **Log income and expenses.** Record entries with an amount, account,
  category, date, and notes. Your running totals update automatically.
- **Manage loans, both ways.** Track money you borrowed and money you lent,
  with person, amount, due date, status, and optional interest and term.
  Record loan payments against an account so balances stay in sync.
- **Account-to-account transfers.** Move money between your own accounts
  without it being counted as income or expense.
- **Credit card cycles.** Set a statement day on a credit card account and mark
  monthly cycles as paid from another account.
- **Editable categories.** Customize income and expense categories to match
  how you actually spend.
- **Local PIN lock.** Optionally protect the app with a numeric PIN stored
  securely on your device.
- **Loan due-date reminders.** With your permission, the app schedules a
  local notification a few days before a loan's due date. Nothing is sent to
  any server.
- **Dashboard at a glance.** See total balance and quick totals for income,
  expenses, and loans.

## Built for personal use

- **Offline-first.** All your data lives in a local database on your phone.
- **No account required.** Open the app and start logging.
- **No ads, no trackers.**
- **Default currency: PHP** (Philippine Peso).

## What this app is not

This is a personal money tracker, not banking software. It does not connect to
your bank, your e-wallet, or any financial institution. It will not import your
transactions automatically, fetch live exchange rates, or sync to the cloud.
Everything is entered and edited by you.

## Backups

Because the app is fully offline, your data is tied to this install. Uninstalling
the app or wiping its storage will permanently remove your entries. A built-in
backup / export feature is on the roadmap but is not part of this version.
